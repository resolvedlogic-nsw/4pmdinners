Overview

What is my.lighthouse.net.au?

This is Lighthouse Church's Pre-Pay and Topup portal. A simple website your family uses to pre-purchase credits to be used for meals, coffee, or Friday Youth/Kids activities.

You don't need to install an app. It works right from any web browser on your phone, tablet, or computer.

Step-by-step

Creating your family account

One account covers your whole family. Both parents' names go on the same account - you don't need separate logins.

1
Go to the portal

Open your browser and visit my.lighthouse.net.au. You'll see the Lighthouse Church credits homepage.

2
Choose the area you want to register for

Select the ministry or service you use most - for example 4pm Dinners or Coffee. Each area has its own register link, but your login will work across the whole site.

3
Click "Register"

On the login page, click the Register link to create a new account.

4
Fill in your family details

Enter both parents' first names, your family surname, and a valid email address. This email will be your login and is how the church will contact you about your account.

5
Choose a 4 digit PIN

Pick a PIN you'll remember. It stays private - church staff cannot see it. You will remain logged in on the same device until you log out.

6
Submit and log in

Once registered, you'll be taken straight to your account. Your starting credit balance will show as zero until credits are added.

Tip Already have an account? Just click Login and enter your email and PIN. Use the Forgot PIN link if you can't remember it.

Transferring what you already have

Getting your existing credits added

If your family already has credits on the church's records from a previous prepaid purchase, they can be moved to your new online account by a volunteer.

1
Create your account first

Your account needs to exist before credits can be added to it. Follow the steps above if you haven't already.

2
Visit the kiosk on Friday/Sunday

Look for a volunteer at the Lighthouse Church kiosk. Let them know you've just registered and have existing credits to transfer.

3
Give them your family name

The volunteer will look up your account by your name and add your credits. You'll see the updated balance in your account straight away.

Note Credits are specific to each area (e.g. coffee credits stay with the coffee cart). If you have credits across different ministries, let the volunteer know and they can transfer each one.

Day-to-day use

Using your credits

Once your account is set up and topped up, using credits is straightforward.

1
Log in at any time

Visit my.lighthouse.net.au and sign in with your email and PIN. Your credit balance is shown on your account dashboard.

2
At the coffee cart or event

Tell the volunteer your family name (or show your QR code on your phone). They'll scan the code or look up your account and deduct the right amount from your balance.

3
Check your balance anytime

Log in to see your current balance and recent transactions. You don't need to be at church to check.

4
Top up when you need to

When your balance is running low, you simply add credits using square payment right in the app, or you can pay cash and a volunteer will manually add the credits.

Common questions

FAQ

Do I need to download an app?

No. The portal is a website - just open it in any browser on your phone or computer.

Can both parents log in?

Yes. It's one shared family account - both parents can log in using the same email and PIN from any device.

I forgot my PIN. What do I do?

On the login page, click the Forgot PIN link and follow the instructions sent to your email address.

My balance doesn't look right. Who do I talk to?

See a volunteer at the kiosk on Sunday and they can look into it for you.

Are my credits refundable?

Please speak with a church volunteer or staff member about credit refund queries - they'll be happy to help.

Can I use credits for more than one ministry area?

Yes - credits are tracked separately per area. You can have a coffee balance, a 4pm Dinners balance, and so on, all under the same account.

Is my information secure?

Your PIN is encrypted and not visible to church staff. Your account details are only used to manage your credits within Lighthouse Church.
